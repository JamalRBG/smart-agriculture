Technology & Agriculture is the New Agriculture.

This year's SMART AGRICULTURE seminar will be held for two weeks starting from 8th January to 22nd January 2024.
The siminar will have four(4) sessions which will cover the whole two weeks. Each sessions will involve different activities where one will learn and/or also be able to share his/her knowledge with the others.
The seminar's venue is Julius Nyerere International Convention Centre ( JNICC) situated in Dar-es-Salaam, Tanzania. This year's agriculture seminar is prepared by the INstitute of Technological Agriculture & Research, (ITAR.).
